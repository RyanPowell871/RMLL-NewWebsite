Why Redesign
The RMLL website has received over 30,000 users from May 22nd 2025 to October 9th 2025,
with the majority visiting on mobile devices (Over 70% - See traffic and insights report
below). Fans, families, and players rely on it for schedules, standings, and league updates.
Currently, there is a large amount of information locked away in PDF downloads and
spreadsheets, making it difficult to access, navigate, and update. The site is currently mobile
“friendly” however the data heavily leans towards the need for a mobile “first” re-design.
A new website will:
● Provide a modern, professional look
● Work seamlessly across phones, tablets, and desktops
● Make forms, budgets, and bylaws easier to access
● Give staff simpler tools to update content
● Optionally include advertising to help cover costs

Options for the Website Build
Option 1: Premium (Custom Build)
A fully custom design tailored to RMLL, with unique layouts, custom graphics, and more
flexibility for future growth.

Option 2: Budget-Friendly (Template-Based)

● New mobile-first homepage featuring schedules, standings, and news
● A searchable document library for bylaws, forms, budgets, and meeting agendas etc.

● A news and media section for announcements, photos, and videos
● Accessibility improvements (WCAG 2.1 compliance)

● Staff training session to manage the site

Optional Add-On: Advertising
Advertising space can be added to the new website.
● Setup Cost: $1,850 one-time
● Ads placed on the homepage, in sidebars, or within news articles
● Estimated Revenue: $300–$700 per month ($3,600–$8,400 per year)
● This could allow the website to pay for itself in 2–4 years

Traffic & Insights Report
Executive Summary
This report provides an overview of the Rocky Mountain Lacrosse League (RMLL) website
traffic and audience insights from May to October 2025. It highlights key usage patterns,
top-performing pages, and opportunities for monetization through advertising.

Key Metrics (May – Oct 2025)
● Active Users: ~31,000
● Engaged Sessions: ~129,000
● Total Event Count: ~1.5M
● Device Breakdown: 73% mobile, 25% desktop, 1% tablet
● Top Cities: Calgary (11K), Edmonton (5.4K), Vancouver (2.5K)

Top Pages
The most visited sections of the site demonstrate where users focus their attention:
1. Junior B Tier I – 49,000 views
2. Jr. B Tier I Schedule – 30,000 views
3. Junior A – 30,000 views
4. Jr. B Tier I Standings – 28,000 views

User Behavior
The site is heavily mobile-first, with nearly three-quarters of users accessing from phones. This
reinforces the need for a fully mobile-optimized redesign.
Traffic is concentrated in Alberta, with Calgary and Edmonton representing the largest user
bases. Vancouver also shows notable engagement, indicating broader regional reach beyond
Alberta.

Ad Revenue Potential
Based on current traffic levels, the RMLL website presents a strong opportunity for ad-based
revenue. Assuming an average CPM of $2.50–$6.00, estimated earnings are:
● Monthly Revenue: $300 – $700 CAD
● Annual Revenue: $3,600 – $8,400 CAD
This means the website could potentially cover its redesign costs within 2–4 years through ad
placements.
